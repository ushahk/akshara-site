Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Your privacy, and specifically the privacy of the young learners who use Akshara, is our utmost priority. This Privacy Policy details exactly how we safeguard that trust.

1. Children's Online Privacy Protection (COPPA)

Akshara is engineered strictly for early childhood use. We are completely COPPA compliant. We do not gather, store, or share any personal identifying information from children who interact with the app.

2. Information Collection

We believe the safest data is the data that is never collected.

  • No Personal Data: We do not track user names, locations, emails, or personal details.
  • No Analytics or Crash Logs: The application does not collect usage patterns or device data via third-party analytics trackers.

3. Permissions & Network Access

The application runs completely offline on your device once downloaded. It requires zero sensitive device permissions and initiates zero outbound API calls, leaving your local profile perfectly secure.

4. Advertisements

There are completely zero advertisements in Akshara. There are no dynamic trackers attempting to target content.
