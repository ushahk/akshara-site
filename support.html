Common Questions

Can my child use the app without an internet connection?

Yes, all core educational packages (vowels, sound clips, images) load immediately on initial installation. Data stays saved fully on your device.

Are there language expansions planned?

We are consistently gathering resources to enable further Indian dialects based on user community interest. Drop us an email!

How can I improve the voice quality in the app?

To enable high-quality (Enhanced) voices, go to your iOS device settings:

  • Settings > Accessibility > Read & Speak > Voices
  • OR
  • Settings > Spoken Content > Voices

Select your language and download an 'Enhanced' voice (e.g., Geeta for Telugu) for the best audio experience.
